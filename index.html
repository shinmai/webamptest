ABOUT NOAH WOLF

WHAT'S MY BACKGROUND?

Noah Wolf was born in Mesa , AZ & moved to California at age 7 , Noah Wolf is an independent American Songwriter & Musical Artist, songwriter and rapper from Southern California. Having developed a unique musical style fusing playful beats with hard-hitting truths,He first gained a global following for his fast songs and deep topics connecting dating, self-discovery and love. Noah released his debut Ep " No Feelings 2 " in December (2018) Streaming over 100k in various countries over the world. Including, but not limited too, South Korea , The Philippines , Canada , United States & Germany.
